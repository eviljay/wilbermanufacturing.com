Wilber Trash Receptacle™

The Wilber Trash Receptacle™ is commercial-quality and very durable. Its all-steel frame is designed to compliment all of our bench and table products. A perfect all-weather, maintenance-free design with a lid that bolts to the top and a side-open door that locks. No one is getting into this receptacle unless you want them to!
