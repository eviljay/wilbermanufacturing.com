Wilber Receptacle
Features & Benefits:
  • Durable steel frame
  • Maintenance free
  • Powder-coated lid
  • Weather-resistant
  • 22 gallon plastic liner

















The heavy-duty Wilber Trash Receptacle™ is made of extruded vinyl and steel creating a weather-resistant receptacle perfect for outdoor public areas including parks, pools, camp grounds and shopping centers. The steel frames are powder coated and can be customized in a variety of colors. The receptacle can be ordered with or without vinyl inserts that may be modified with colored end caps. The Wilber Trash Receptacle™ includes a 22 gallon plastic trash liner and steel powder-coated lid.
