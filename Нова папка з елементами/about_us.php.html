About Wilber Manufacturing

Wilber Manufacturing was founded in 1963 and manufactured self-storing aluminum storm windows and storm doors for more than 40 years - holding the contract for local and national weatherization programs. In the middle 1990's the company entered the replacement window and door market. It was also in the middle 1990's that Wilber Manufacturing began making site amenities - first for their local Czech Festival and later for distribution nationally. Seeing growth of its site amenities product lines, Wilber Manufacturing sold its Wilber Window and Door business in 2006 to operate solely as a manufacturer of its Wilber-brand site furnishings and custom-made outdoor furniture.
